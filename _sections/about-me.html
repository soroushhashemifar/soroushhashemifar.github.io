Since the age of 16, Soroush (/soˈruːʃ/) showed a great passionate for computer related fields, specifically computer networks and Cisco certifications (including CCNA and CCNP) for computer networks and computer programming. Growing up in a small society, a family that encourages him to follow his dreams, Soroush managed to graduate in Computer Engineering at Razi University with a GPA of 17.28 on scale of 20 and be among top 3 students of the faculty for 4 consecutive years. He was part of the High Performance Computing (HPC) lab as a research assistant until the beginning of 2021, under supervision of Prof. Amir Rajabzadeh. Currently, he's a M.Sc. student in software engineering at Iran University of Science and Technology (IUST) under supervision of Prof. Saeed Parsa. His research interests include Recommender Systems, Deep Neural Networks, Computer Vision, NLP, and Explainable AI.
