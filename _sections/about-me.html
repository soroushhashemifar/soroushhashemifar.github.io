Since the age of 12, Soroush (/soˈruːʃ/) showed a great passionate for computer related fields, specifically robotics. It all started with a summer boot-camp on creating basic robots for teenagers, and continued with Cisco certifications (including CCNA and CCNP) for computer networks and computer programming.

Growing up in a small society, a family that encourages him to follow his dreams, Soroush managed to graduate in Computer Engineering at Razi University with a GPA of 17.26 on scale of 20 and be among top 3 students of the faculty for 4 consecutive years. At the moment, he's a M.Eng. student in software engineering at Iran University of Science and Technology and he's researching on fault localization and repairing in deep neural networks. His research interests include computer vision, reinforcement learning, as well as self-supervised learning and representation learning.

Soroush’s dedication to artificial intelligence encourages him to actively look for opportunities to expand his knowledge in computer vision and other interesting areas of artificial intelligence, and explore various architectures to gain a deep understanding about deep networks.
