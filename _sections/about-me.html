Since the age of 12, Soroush (/soˈruːʃ/) showed a great passionate for computer related fields, specifically robotics. It all started with a summer boot-camp on creating basic robots for teenagers, and continued with Cisco certifications (including CCNA and CCNP) for computer networks and computer programming.
Growing up in a small society, a family that encourages him to follow his dreams, Soroush managed to graduate in Computer Engineering at Razi University with a GPA of 17.28 on scale of 20 and be among top 3 students of the faculty for 4 consecutive years. In 2019, his Bachelor's thesis became one of top 30 Bachelor's projects in the field of IoT inside Iran. He was part of the High Performance Computing (HPC) lab as a research assistant until the beginning of 2021, under supervision of Prof. Amir Rajabzadeh.
Currently, he's a M.Sc. student in software engineering at Iran University of Science and Technology (IUST). Also, he has joined the Reverse Engineering lab at IUST as a research assistant since the beginning of 2022, under supervision of Prof. Saeed Parsa. His thesis is focused on detecting and repairing backdoored neural networks by neural trojans.
His research interests include deep neural networks, computer vision, software engineering. His particular interests are in various aspects of representation learning, object detection/recognition, fault localization, and neural network testing.
